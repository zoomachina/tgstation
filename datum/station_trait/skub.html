skub
/datum/station_trait/skub

The number of links to perform. Combined with 50/50 the probability of the link being triangular, the boundaries of any given on-station, non-secure closet being linked are as high as 1 in 7/8 and as low as 1 in 16-17, nearing an a mean of 1 in 9 to 11/12 the more repetitions are done.

There are more than 220 roundstart closets on meta, around 150 of which aren't secure, so, about 13 to 17 closets will be affected by this most of the times. A trait that lets players choose whether they want pro-skub or anti-skub (or neither), and receive the appropriate equipment.
